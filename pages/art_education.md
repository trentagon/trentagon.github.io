# Art Education

## Coyote Central
I teach an original class called **Generative Design: Making Art with Code** at the [Coyote Central](https://www.coyotenorth.org){:target="\_blank"} youth arts organization in Seattle, WA. The class is 20 hours long, taught over 5 days, with 7-8 students aged 10-15 and no prior coding experience. It is remarkable how much they learn and what they were able to accomplish with their final projects.
