<p>I am originally from Southern New Jersey, just outside of Philadelphia. I am a passionate fan of Philadelphia sports teams, <a href="https://www.espn.com/pdf/2016/0406/nba_hinkie_redact.pdf" target="_blank">as painful as it may be</a>, and I have a particular interest in analyzing and visualizing sports data. In my free time, I am an avid soccer player, runner, and <a href="/pages/trents_book_club.html">reader</a>.</p>

<p>To me, planets are fascinating examples of systems with complex behavior that emerges from a large number of interacting parts. The mathematical structure behind this behavior can be found elsewhere in <a href="https://oimo.io/works/life/" target="_blank">unexpected</a> <a href="https://www.arthurganson.com/wishbone" target="_blank">places</a>. I search for these <a href="https://boids.dan.onl" target="_blank">other</a> <a href="https://apps.amandaghassaei.com/gpu-io/examples/physarum/" target="_blank">places</a> by exploring the intersection of <a href="/pages/art_gallery.html">art</a>, mathematics, and science in pursuit of the computational beauty of nature.</p>
